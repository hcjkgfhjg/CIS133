  • Pet Selection

A dog can either add stress to your life or make your life better. That's why it is
important to choose the right pet for your home.

Here are some tips that can help you to select the right type of dog for your family.

1. If you have young kids, consider their well-being by choosing nicer dogs such as Beagle.
Your kid might mistreat the dog and could lead to biting. Avoid getting agressive breeds
such as German Shepard or Chihuahua as they tend to bite.
