A Dog's Life was established in 2012 when our owner Prakesh retired from his day job.
Here at A Dog's Life, our mission is to make sure every dog looks beautiful leaving our salon.
Not only that, grooming your dogs can benefit them in many ways such as:

  1. Improve your dog's hygene, making it less susceptible to bacteria and infection
  2. Trimming their nails can improve their posture and prevent bone deformation
  3. Trimmed coat can help them to shed less, have a healthier and shiny coat
  4. By grooming your dog regularly, we can detect any skin or health issue that might
    have developed, therefore preventing it from becoming more serious

Appointments at A Dog's Life fill up quickly, so we recommend calling in advance to reserve
a spot for your pet. We are open every day from 8 A.M to 6 P.M so give us a call and we'll glam your pet up!
